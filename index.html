Hugo Reichardt

I am a tenure-track Junior Researcher at the Centre de Recerca en Economia Internacional (CREi), an Affiliated Professor at the Barcelona School of Economics (BSE), and a Research Affiliate at the Center for Economic Policy Research (CEPR).

My main research interests are in macroeconomics and economic history.

I received a PhD in economics from LSE in 2024.
